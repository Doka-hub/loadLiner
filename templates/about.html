About us

Logistics Breaking Boundaries

Since 1961, the Saoud family has been an integral part of the food industry, and FreezPak specializes in transforming cold chain logistics with tailored solutions to meet diverse industry needs.

2000s

FreezPak offers full cold storage solutions, merging warehousing and logistics for efficient supply chain management. Established in 1960, the Saoud family, known for swift distribution, entered cold storage logistics in 2001. Celebrated as the fastest cold storage shipper globally, FreezPak maintains optimal temperature conditions with cutting-edge technology, preserving product quality during storage.
