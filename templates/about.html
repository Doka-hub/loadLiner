About Us

Logistics Without Limits

{# Born in 1961 from the Saoud family’s deep roots in the food trade, Load Liner#} {# now re-imagines cold-chain logistics with bespoke, industry-specific solutions.#} Founded in June 2023, Load Liner Logistics was built on a clear mission: to bring flexibility, speed, and transparency to modern freight logistics. As a new-generation carrier and dispatching partner, we leverage technology and a hands-on approach to deliver reliable, end-to-end shipping solutions across the U.S.

A Bit of History

1960s

Humble Roots. A single 5 000-sq ft produce warehouse in New Jersey set the cornerstone for today’s cold-chain network.

1970s

Rolling Cold. First fleet of refrigerated trucks connected farms to regional grocers overnight.

1990s

New Generation. Second-gen leadership invested in barcode inventory and 24/7 operations.

2000s

Rebrand & Automation. Relaunched as Load Liner and opened the first fully automated freezer with AS/RS cranes.

2010s

Nationwide Reach. Added West Coast and Gulf mega-sites, giving clients two-day coverage across the U.S.

2020s

Greener Future. Solar roofs, hydrogen forklifts and AI route-planning push us—and our partners—toward net-zero supply chains.

Load Liner delivers end-to-end cold-storage solutions, pairing smart warehousing with agile logistics for seamless supply-chain flow. Since 1960 the Saoud family has been synonymous with rapid distribution; our 2001 move into temperature-controlled logistics added cutting-edge tech that keeps every pallet in perfect condition.
