At Load Liner Logistics Inc , your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when using our website or services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Name, phone number, and email address
Company name, MC/DOT numbers
Location and contact information
Website usage data (cookies, IP address, browser type)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
Coordinate logistics and dispatching services
Improve website functionality and user experience
Fulfill regulatory and legal requirements

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your information. We may share it only with:

Trusted third-party service providers
Brokers, carriers, and shippers directly involved in logistics operations
Government agencies when required by law

4. Data Security

We use appropriate administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar tools to improve functionality and analyze website traffic. You may disable cookies through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

Access, update, or delete your personal information
Request details on how your data is used
To exercise your rights, please contact us.

7. Consent:

8. Updates to the Privacy Policy:

9. Contact Us:
