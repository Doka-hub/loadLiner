Trichinosis

Ensuring Safe and Certified Products

FreezPak Logistics offers a comprehensive range of cold storage and supply chain solutions across its facilities, tailored to meet the unique needs of the food industry, including specialized trichinosis services. These services focus on ensuring the safe handling, storage, and distribution of pork and other meats that require specific processes to eliminate the risk of trichinosis, a parasitic infection.

At FreezPak, we combine expertise and cutting-edge technology to ensure your pork products meet the highest standards of safety and quality. Our trichinosis certification services help you navigate complex regulations while maintaining the integrity of your goods. With our reliable and efficient solutions, you can confidently deliver certified products to your customers.
